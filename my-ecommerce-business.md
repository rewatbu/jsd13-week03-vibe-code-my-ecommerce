# Project Overview

## Project Name: Coffee Bean Online Store

### Project Description:
The Coffee Bean Online Store is an e-commerce application that allows customers to browse and purchase premium coffee beans online. Customers can search products by origin and roast level, place orders, make payments, and view their order history. Administrators manage products, inventory, users, and customer orders through the system.

### Main Features:

- User registration and login
- Browse and search coffee products
- Product management
- Inventory management
- Online ordering
- Payment tracking
- Order management
- Customer order history

### Database Collections:

* Users – Customer and administrator information
* Products – Coffee bean catalog
* Inventory – Product stock levels
* Orders – Customer purchase records
